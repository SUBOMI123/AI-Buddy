# App-specific tuning

Once general-purpose guidance is reliable (V1) and visual grounding is working (V2), invest in app-specific knowledge:

- **App detection** — identify which app is in focus (Excel, Figma, VS Code, etc.)
- **App-specific prompts** — tailored guidance that knows the app's vocabulary and workflows
- **DOM assist (web only)** — for web apps, optionally read DOM for higher precision
- **Partial automation** — for repetitive steps, offer to perform clicks/actions directly
- **Community contributions** — let power users contribute app-specific guidance packs
